About LuxWatch

LuxWatch is a mock-up e-commerce website developed in 2022 by Justin Alarcio during his senior high school Empowerment Technology subject. The project was designed to replicate a luxury online watch store, featuring world-renowned brands such as Rolex, Omega, TAG Heuer, and Patek Philippe.

Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, LuxWatch demonstrated both technical skill and creative design, presenting a polished interface that reflected the prestige of the products it showcased.

As the group leader and lead programmer, Justin directed the project’s development and ensured its successful completion. Under the evaluation of their IT instructor, Mr. Mark Gil Mifuel, LuxWatch was recognized with the Best Website Award within their academic strand.

This recognition not only highlighted Justin’s programming and leadership skills but also established a strong foundation for his continuing journey in the field of Information Technology.
